In this project we design a quadcopter that can do something that's never been attempted: land like a bird. A robotic end effector is mounted on a Sky Specs quadcopter

New, Unique, and Different Challenges

I designed and developed the architecture for leather composite parts which was used in three flagship products. As a NUD owner I had to establish a new mechanical design for leather parts such that they retained their quality feel but add little thickness to the phone and remain durable for several years. This also required pioneering new manufacturing techniques, testing specifications, and material sourcing channels. I worked with Horween, a historical tannery based in Chicago, and manufacturing centers in Shenzhen, China to perfect and scale the product.

During the prototyping phase of the project, I was an on-site interface for the China base suppliers to root cause manufacturing issues, drive schedules, and support ramp up of production parts. I applied Six-Sigma principles to decrease defective parts below 0.001%.
